How to set up an account

  1. Download the app on IOS or Android in the app store.
  2. Click on the app and let WhatsApp connect to your contacts.
  3. Fill out your name and pick a profile picture for your profile and your done! (You can connect your Facebook account to your WhatsApp if you prefer).
